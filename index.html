Why You're Here

Hello and thank you for stopping by to help keep Fred's dream alive. You see, Fred has been running his donut shop for quite some time now and he's ready to hang up the apron. However, Fred's love for donuts has not dwindled in the slightest and this is where your assistance is needed! I've been contracted by Fred to seek help in making donuts for his shop while he enjoys retirement and I'm going to make the process super simple. Let's give him a hand!
